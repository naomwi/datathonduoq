## Promo Calendar Parity Finding
- Raw promotions stop at 2022, so 2023-2024 future promo features are generated by policy, not known observations.
- `promotions.csv` has a strict odd/even pattern:
  - Odd years 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2021 include `Rural Special` and `Urban Blowout`.
  - Even years 2014, 2016, 2018, 2020, 2022 do not include those extras.
- Forecast period includes 2023 odd year and 2024 even year.
- Current future policy uses recent 2-year month-day averages, so it smears the 2021 extra promos and 2022 no-extra pattern into both 2023 and 2024.
- This likely underweights 2023 extra promos and overweights 2024 Rural Special.
